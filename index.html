Future of Coding

Welcome to the podcast and open-journal research project to create the future of coding. My name is Steve Krouse.

Every week I release a new podcast, alternating between recapping my own research over the past two weeks and interviewing programming experts.

You can read more about this project here.

You can also join our Slack group , which has monthly meetups in NYC.
